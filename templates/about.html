OWO

Introduction to the Project

This project uses tools that understand and process human language to help make the internet safer by spotting harmful content. It carefully considers how people talk to each other online to tell the difference between harmful and harmless speech. The goal is to create fair methods that do not worsen existing problems while respecting free speech. This work also makes it easier to find and report online harm, helping to build a safer online community. The project addresses the challenges of dealing with complex language and content online, explaining the careful approaches needed to manage online content.
